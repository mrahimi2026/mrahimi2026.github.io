مرحله ۳: نمونه ایمیل (قسمت‌های مشخص‌شده را تغییر دهید)

✏️ قسمت‌هایی که با رنگ مشخص شده‌اند را با اطلاعات خودتان جایگزین کنید. 

Subject: Urgent Appeal for Action in Support of the Iranian People

Dear Senator/Representative نام خانوادگی نماینده,

My name is نام شما, and I am a constituent from
شهر و ایالت شما. As Iranian American innovator, I am  deeply committed to advancing technology, sustainability, and the values of freedom, democracy, and human rights that underpin our nation. 

I am writing to express my concern about the ongoing crisis in Iran, where the Islamic Republic regime has implemented widespread internet restrictions to suppress information about peaceful protests. Reports of significant loss of life (more than 30000 casualties) and systemic challenges to civil liberties have raised serious questions about the situation. 

The regime’s actions have had profound regional and global implications, including links to violence against U.S. personnel. I believe the U.S. has a critical role to play in supporting democratic aspirations and human rights worldwide. 

President Trump’s statements, particularly following Prince Reza Pahlavi’s call for civic engagement, resonated with many in Iran. His emphasis on resilience and action inspired hope for progress. As he noted in a recent message: “Iranian Patriots, KEEP PROTESTING – TAKE OVER YOUR INSTITUTIONS!!! HELP IS ON ITS WAY. PRESIDENT DONALD J. TRUMP” 

I respectfully call upon you to: 

Advocate for decisive action to address the Islamic Republic’s authoritarian rule and support the Iranian people in their pursuit of democratic change, including engaging in global negotiations with world leaders to reduce financial, military, and technological support for the regime as well as targeted military actions against regime’s repressive apparatus to help empower the Iranian people in their civil protests.  
Support efforts to provide temporary internet access to millions of Iranians subjected to a digital blackout, ensuring they can communicate, share information, and access critical resources. 
Consider endorsing Prince Reza Pahlavi as a potential leader to guide a transitional government, as he has proposed a peaceful, secular path to shift Iran from the Islamic Republic to a democratic system that upholds territorial integrity and civil liberties. 
Your leadership in this matter could save countless lives and pave the way for a stable, prosperous Iran aligned with global democratic principles. We stand ready to provide further insights from our community. 

Thank you for your attention. 

Sincerely,
نام شما
